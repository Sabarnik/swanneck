The Swanneck Story

From Innovation to Global Impact

Swanneck Agile Waters Pvt. Ltd. was born from a singular, audacious idea: What if water could deliver the complex taste and sensory experience of the world's finest spirits — without a drop of alcohol?

Through years of research and development, our team of beverage scientists and water engineers developed a proprietary maturation process that transforms purified water at a molecular level. The result is a patent-pending technology that is truly first-of-its-kind in the global beverage industry.

Our product range spans matured water beverages that emulate whisky, brandy, wine, and beer — alongside health drinks, flavoured water, energy drinks, mineral water, and specialty concentrates. We serve both B2B and B2C segments, with our primary market in India and expansion planned across the European Union, UAE, United States, China, and other strategic global markets.
